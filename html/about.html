About

The Story

As men, there are few monumental milestones in our lives. Learning how to shave and learning how to drive are common practise but we are not told about the importance of smelling sensational. I became engrossed in the fragrance community during the latter stages of 2018 going into 2019. This was around the time that Viktor and Rolfs Spicebomb had started to take over the market. It is a very distinctive fragrance overflowing with spice and caught my attention immediately. I bought the gift set from Boots alongside a small bottle of the equally as sweet Stronger with You by Emporio Armani and thought I was the best smelling man in the world. This was in fact the case for the Autumn and early Spring months as it didn't overpower everyone, however, by April I had noticed that I had made a big mistake. I will explain more about this mistake on the page titled Seasons . The aim for me will be to educate you on the mistakes that I made in the 2+ years that I have been involved in fragrance. It will also document future mistakes that I will eventually make as I continue to learn about scents. It will act as a guide for men who are just getting into scents, looking at the best scents for the best occasions and at an array of prices

Hopefully you learn a lot, try some scents and enjoy the journey.

Ashley Small
